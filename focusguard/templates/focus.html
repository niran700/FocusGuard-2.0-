Pomodoro Technique

Focus: 25 min Break: 5 min

Work with full concentration for 25 minutes and then take a 5-minute break. After completing 4 cycles, take a longer break of 15–20 minutes.

✓ Helps maintain focus and prevents mental fatigue.

📚

52/17 Rule

Focus: 52 min Break: 17 min

Focus deeply on work for 52 minutes followed by a 17-minute break. This cycle balances productivity and rest.

✓ Maintains high productivity and reduces burnout.

⚡

90-Minute Deep Work

Focus: 90 min Break: 20-30 min

Work without distractions for 90 minutes and then take a longer restorative break.

✓ Encourages deep work and maximum cognitive performance.
